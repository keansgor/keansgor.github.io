What are the requirement to incorporate Sdn Bhd?
The requirements to form a Sdn Bhd or private limited company are:
1. A minimum of one director
2. A minimum of one shareholder
3. A company secretary who is certified and licensed under Company Commission of Malaysia (SSM)
